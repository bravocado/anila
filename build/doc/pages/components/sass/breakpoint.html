--- title: Breakpoint layout: build/doc/layouts/sass.html --- *** # Breakpoint *** See our available values : ```scss $sm-range: (0, 640px) !default; $md-range: (641px, 1024px) !default; $lg-range: (1025px, 1440px) !default; $xl-range: (1441px, 1920px) !default; $xxl-range: (1921px) !default; ```
- Small range for mobile-only. - Medium range for tablet-only. - Large range for a laptop or a standard desktop screen size. - Extra large for a bigger desktop screen size. - Extra extra large for a screen size more than an usual desktop screen size.

We also cover for landscape and potrait media queries. ```scss $landscape: "#{$screen} and (orientation: landscape)" !default; $portrait: "#{$screen} and (orientation: portrait)" !default; ```
